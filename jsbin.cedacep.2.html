Ingredients

Chocolate Mud Cake

  • Unsalted butter 2 sticks
  • good dark chocolate 1/3 of a cup
  • 2 sups Custer sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • cocoa powder1/2 a cup
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 a cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoonvanilla exract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 150 C
  2. melt butter and dark chocolate in a microwave.
    • mix every 30 second until smooth.
  3. add both type sugars into the chocolate mix
  4. add eggs and mix to combine
  5. add cocoa powder, flour and baking soda and mix gently
  6. Add the milk and the vanilla
  7. stir until chcolate batter is combined and smooth
  8. Pour cake butter into greased baking tin/ tin layered with parchment paper.

The cake should be moist, smell like a chocolate factory would. You could decorate it however your heart desires. Remember, its about making you happy.
